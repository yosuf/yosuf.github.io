Software

Along with other things in life, software has become one of those essential things we can hardly live without. Software is literally everywhere. Wifi, smart phones, millions of apps, cars, traffic management systems, banking & financial systems, education & medical systems, webshops and what not is made of software. Software continues to grow and fill other aspects of our lives. In fact, some software is “so old” that many software systems must be replaced or re-written because they are not of this time anymore. -Yosuf Haydary
